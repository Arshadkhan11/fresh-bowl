About Us

Fresh Bowl emerged as a mission to share our take on the traditional dishes of Vietnam.

We are a locally and family-owned restaurant, in Austin, Texas, with a promise to offer our customers an insight into the dishes of our homeland. Predominantly inspired by our southern Vietnamese appetite, our restaurant is also known for its take on Bun Bo Hue and sweet-flavored pho.

Fresh bowl is also pleased to introduce a key element of pho: fresh rice noodles. We wish to return the memories of the pho that 1st generation immigrants, like us, grew up eating and perhaps create new flavorful memories with those new to this trip down memory lane.
